Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my AI tool to the directory?

Submitting your AI tool is completely free and straightforward. Simply click the "Submit Tool" button in our navigation bar, fill out the required information including tool name, description, category, pricing model, and website URL. Our team reviews all submissions within 24-48 hours to ensure quality and accuracy before publishing.

What criteria do you use to evaluate AI tools?

We evaluate tools based on functionality, user experience, innovation, reliability, pricing value, and community feedback. Our expert team tests each tool thoroughly and considers factors like ease of use, feature completeness, customer support, and overall value proposition before featuring them in our directory.

How often is the directory updated with new tools?

We update our directory daily with new AI tools and regularly refresh existing listings with the latest information, features, and pricing updates. Our team monitors the AI landscape continuously to ensure we feature the most current and relevant tools available in the market.

Can I get featured or promoted if I have a premium tool?

While we don't accept payment for featured placements, we do offer various promotional opportunities for high-quality tools. Premium tools that receive strong community feedback and demonstrate exceptional value may be featured in our weekly highlights, newsletter, or social media channels. Contact our team to learn more about partnership opportunities.

How do the upvote and rating systems work?

Our upvote system allows registered users to show appreciation for tools they've used and found valuable. Each user can upvote a tool only once, ensuring fair representation. Ratings are based on user feedback, expert reviews, and our internal evaluation criteria, helping the community identify the most reliable and effective AI solutions.
