Submission Guidelines

Before submitting your tool, please ensure it meets the following criteria:

  • Your tool must be directly related to artificial intelligence, machine learning, or automation.
  • The tool must be functional and accessible to users (not just a concept or idea).
  • You must have the rights to submit the tool (you are the owner, creator, or authorized representative).
  • The tool's website must be professional, functional, and provide clear information about the product.
  • Tools with malicious intent, adult content, or that violate any laws will not be accepted.
  • We reserve the right to edit or reject submissions that do not meet our quality standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my submission to be reviewed?

Our team typically reviews submissions within 3-5 business days. You will receive an email notification once your tool has been approved or if we need additional information.

Can I submit a tool that I don't own?

You can only submit tools that you own or have explicit permission to promote. If you're submitting a tool on behalf of someone else, please provide proof of authorization in the description field.

Is there a fee for submitting a tool?

No, submitting a tool to Latest Ai Tools is completely free. We believe in providing a platform for all AI tools to be discovered, regardless of budget.

How can I update my tool's information after submission?

If you need to update your tool's information, please contact us with the tool name and details of the changes you'd like to make. Our team will assist you in updating the listing.
